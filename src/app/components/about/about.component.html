About the BMI Calculator

The Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator helps you understand whether your weight is within a healthy range. BMI is a simple calculation based on your height and weight. It provides an indication of whether you are underweight, have a normal weight, are overweight, or are obese.

About

Please note that BMI is a general guide and may not consider other factors such as muscle mass or body composition. It is always a good idea to consult with a healthcare provider for a more accurate assessment of your health.

How is BMI Calculated?

The BMI formula is:
BMI = weight (kg) / height² (m²)
Our calculator automatically converts your height from centimeters to meters to compute the BMI.
