Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a measurement that uses height and weight to estimate body fat.

Is BMI a reliable measure of health?

While BMI is a useful screening tool, it does not directly measure body fat or account for muscle mass. Consult a healthcare professional for a complete assessment.
